Rubik's Cube Solving

Using Deep Learning

Our project presents an innovative solution for solving the Rubik's Cube using cutting-edge technology. Leveraging visual recognition through camera scanning and virtual line drawing, we've created an interactive experience that guides users step-by-step to solve the iconic puzzle. Our approach integrates advanced algorithms like Kociemba's to analyze cube configurations and determine optimal solutions. By seamlessly blending computer vision, deep learning, and algorithmic thinking, our project not only offers a unique way to conquer the Rubik's Cube but also showcases the potential of technology to enhance traditional challenges. Our endeavor exemplifies the fusion of creativity and technical expertise to revolutionize puzzle-solving methods.

Instructions

1.Set up the camera and ensure proper calibration for accurate color recognition.

2.Activate the camera scanning feature in the application.

3.Position the Rubik's Cube in good lighting within the camera's view.

4.Capture images of all six sides of the Rubik's Cube using the camera.

5.Utilize the project's algorithm to identify the colors of each cubelet.

6.Verify the detected colors match the actual cube colors.

7.Apply the algorithm to virtually draw lines on the cube based on recognized colors.

8.Integrate the Kociemba solving algorithm with the recognized cube configuration.

9.Display the solution steps visually using the drawn virtual lines on the cube.

10.Follow the virtual lines to execute each step of the solution.

11.The application updates the virtual lines with each completed step.

Ensure the cube's sides are consistently colored after completing all steps.
